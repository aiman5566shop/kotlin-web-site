Code of Conduct

Follow these simple rules to collaborate efficiently: be kind, be someone that other people want to be around, do not insult or be rude to other attendees. Kotlin User Groups should provide a friendly, safe and welcoming environment for all, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, ability, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, and religion (or lack there of)
